Full description:
This application adds exo Roussillon buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from @exo_Roussillon on Twitter.

exo Roussillon serves Delson, Saint-Constant and Sainte-Catherine in Québec, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM).
https://rtm.quebec/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-roussillon-citrous-bus-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM), the Autorité Régionale de Transport Métropolitain (ARTM) and exo Roussillon.

exo Roussillon was formely know as RTM Roussillon sector and CIT Roussillon.
exo was formely know as Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM) and Agence Métropolitaine de Transport (AMT).
